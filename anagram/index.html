Anagram Game

Hi! Welcome to the anagram game!

You are given a word from the programming world. There are 8 words total. The rules of the game are very simple:

  • 1. The words must exist in the dictionary
  • 2. The words must be nouns containing at least three letters
  • 3. The words must be divided by a SPACE
  • 4. In order to move to the next word you have to guess at least 5 smaller ones. More are also welcome!
